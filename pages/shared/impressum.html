Data Protection Declaration

The protection of your personal data is of particular concern to us. We therefore process your data exclusively on the basis of legal provisions (GDPR, TMG). In this data protection declaration, we inform you about the most important aspects of data processing on our website.

1. Contact with us

If you contact us via the form on the website or by email, your data will be stored for six months for the purpose of processing the inquiry and for any follow-up questions. We will not pass on this data without your consent.

2. Access Data / Server Log Files

The website operator or page provider collects data about access to the page and stores them as "server log files". The following data is recorded: IP address, browser type, pages visited, date and time of access. This data is used exclusively for statistical evaluations and to improve the website.

3. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user-friendliness and enable certain functions. If you do not wish this, you can set up your browser to inform you about the setting of cookies or deactivate this.

4. Your Rights

You basically have the rights to information, correction, deletion, restriction, data portability and objection. If you believe that the processing of your data violates data protection law, you can complain to the responsible supervisory authority.
